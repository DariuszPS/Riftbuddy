What to expect?

Card Search

Find any Riftbound card instantly. Search by name, keyword, effect text, or artist. Filter by domain, rarity, type, and energy cost

Deck Builder

Build, edit, and save your decks without losing your work. Live legality checker, energy curve visualization, and one-click import/export.

Card Scanner

Point your camera at any physical card and RiftBuddy identifies it instantly. Jump straight to Card Detail, add it to a deck, or save it to your collection.

Event Locator

Find Nexus Nights, Summoner Skirmishes, and Regional Qualifiers near you. See dates, locations, and get directions all without leaving the app.

Glossary

Every keyword, explained. Tap any keyword directly from a card's effect text and instantly see what it means.

Tier List / Meta

See what's winning. Browse community picks and tournament results, explore top decklists, and copy any build straight into your deck builder.
